Privacy Policy for HavoRadar

Last updated: November 2025

1. Introduction

HavoRadar is an air quality monitoring application. We do not collect or store any personal data.

2. Data We Collect

The application does not ask for or store any personal information. Location is only used to show local air quality and never leaves your device.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following services:

  • AirVisual API – to retrieve air quality information.
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging – to send alert notifications (AQI updates).

4. Analytics

This app does not use third-party analytics.
